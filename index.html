Basketball Shot Angle Detector

1. Click "Upload Video" to select a basketball shot video.

2. The video will appear below. Click the Play/Pause button to control playback.

3. As the video plays, the app will attempt to detect your arm and display the angle between your upper arm and forearm in real-time.

4. When you see the moment of ball release, click "Log Current Angle" to record that specific angle and timestamp.

Note: Generating a new video file with overlaid annotations is a complex task for web browsers and is not supported in this client-side demo. The annotations are shown live on the video.
